Shanxi Lutu was incorporated in 2006 in Shanxi Province as a domestic company, and was acquired by Hong Kong GVBT Corporation and converted into a wholly foreign-owned enterprise (“WFOE”) in 2012.

GVBT’s mission is to strive for production of fertilizer products in China which are ecologically, socially and economically friendly and that our production activities and products are based on the principles of Organic Agriculture.
