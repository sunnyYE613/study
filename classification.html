Currently, our fertilizers are classified as microbial fertilizer, organic microbial fertilizer and compound microbial fertilizers under the “Measures for the Administration of Fertilizer Registration”. In other words, our series of products are fertilizer products which contain specific living microorganisms and have specific effects when applied in agricultural production. The specific effects include the provision of nutrients to soil and crops and beneficial effects on the metabolism of plants.
