    and Development

A scientist of Shanxi Lutu, Professor Zhao Liangqi, of the Microbiology Department of the Shanxi University, in collaboration with a senior staff of Shanxi Lu, Mr. Mu Yaogui, who graduated from Shanxi Agriculture University, have spent years developing a mixture of microorganisms to manufacture bio-fertilizers from locally available mineral shale which originally cannot be used as fertilizers. They arrived at a mixture of various strains of microorganisms and invented a new product of mineral-based bio-fertilizers (the “MBF”).

Shanxi Lutu currently holds a patent in PRC on the production method of the MBF produced (Patent number: ZL200910073705.5) and hence possesses the microbial technology to manufacture MBF directly from mineral shale.

In 2011, Shanxi Lutu purchased a piece of industrial land which is 34,256 square meters to expand its production capacity. In the same year, an approval to construct a 500,000 tons per annum of fertilizers production was granted by the Chinese Government. After several rounds of corporate transactions and fundraising, Shanxi Lutu is now a WFOE ultimately owned by shareholders based in HK with the current shareholding structure as described in the following section. At present, Shanxi Lutu has a production capacity of 100,000 tons per annum.
