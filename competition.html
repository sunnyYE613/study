Our company’s developing experience and technology combine to obtain several competitive advantages:

i. Nature of MBF

• Through the life activities of microorganisms, Shanxi Lutu’s MBF are able to promote crop growth, improve the resistance of crops to pests, improve the quality of crops and provide soil amendment directly or indirectly.

• Our MBF can reduce the usage of chemical fertilizers and pesticides significantly and are more efficient in decomposing the existing chemical fertilizers and pesticides accumulated in soil.

ii. Patents were granted for MBF production

• We obtained a patent of the production method of MBF in 2012.

• We also has two other patent applications pending the approval of State Intellectual Property Office and one patent application which is in the process of PCT and is prepared to be submitted to United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) for registration.

• Once the approval procedures of these patent applications are completed, we can benefit from more tax reductions under the policies of the Chinese government.

iii. Our Company has well proven and recognised bio-fertilizer products.

• Products of our company have been tested in several locations in China with local farmers and users, and have produced favourable results.

• Products from existing competitors are not of comparable quantity and quality.

• Products from existing competitors may not be designed in a user-friendly way to consider ease of use.

iv. Our company does not currently have other significant competitors in the market.

• We has already established our phase I production facilities of 100,000 tons per annum and operations,

• We have not yet seen another significant competitor that has comparable scale or product quality.

v. Our research and development capability allows further expansion to other parts of China.

• We have research and development capability and have successfully landed on breakthroughs with further patent applications in 2014 after the initial patent.

• We are able to perform studies on the feasibility of utilising mineral resources in other parts of China to establish production facilities outside of its initial facilities.

vi. Our MBF benefits from China’s State policies of enhancing the potential of organic and bio- fertilizers market in China.

• “The Action Plan of Achieving Zero Growth of Chemical Fertilizers Usage at 2020” promulgated by the Ministry of Agriculture dated February, 2015.

• “CPC Central Committee on National Economic and Social Development Five-Year Plan” dated November, 2015.

• “Certain Opinions on Implementing New Ideas of Development and Accelerating Modernization of Agriculture to Realize the Goal of Comprehensive Water Conservancy” dated December, 2015 and “Prevention of Soil Pollution Action Plan” dated May, 2016.

• These State policies highlight the conservation of agricultural environment and emphasize that preventive measures tackling agricultural pollution should be stepped up and zero growth of the usage of chemical fertilizers should be achieved.

vii. Our products will be benefited from the Tax Reductions Policy in favor of organic fertilizer products in China.

• In January, 2016, it is mentioned in “Notice Concerning the Clarification of Enforcement Standard of Organic Fertilizers” published by the State Administration of Taxation that taxpayers can be exempted from VAT if they engage in the production, distribution or retail of organic fertilizer products. Accordingly, we are exempted from paying the VAT tax, which is currently 17%.

• In addition, after Shanxi Lutu has obtained the above-mentioned two patents, it is believed that our company will be categorized as “Advanced Technology Companies” and can benefit from profit tax reductions from the current rate of 25% to 15%.

viii. Our company is poised to becoming one of the largest mineral-based bio--fertilizer and compound bio--fertilizer manufacturers with consistent product quality and competent quality control.

• We believe that there are not many competitors within China which can provide the same comprehensive fertilizing solution to farmers with complete provision of macro nutrients (N, P, K), secondary nutrients and micro nutrients.
